# Yapform Content Guide

## Product Overview

Yapform is an AI-powered quiz and form builder for marketers. Core capabilities:

- **AI Quiz Builder** -- Generate personalized product recommendation quizzes, lead qualification quizzes, and personality-style assessments with AI-assisted question logic
- **Smart Form Builder** -- Conditional logic forms, multi-step flows, and embedded lead capture forms with real-time validation
- **Personalization Engine** -- Dynamic results pages, personalized product recommendations, and segmented follow-up sequences based on quiz responses
- **Zero-Party Data Collection** -- Capture declared preferences, purchase intent, and audience insights directly from users through interactive experiences

**Key Differentiators:** AI-powered question generation, built-in personalization logic, native e-commerce integrations (Shopify, WooCommerce, Klaviyo), conversion-optimized templates designed for DTC and lead gen use cases.

## Brand Voice

### Tone Rules

| Dimension | Target | Avoid |
|-----------|--------|-------|
| Confidence | Data-backed authority | Arrogance, hyperbole ("revolutionary", "game-changing") |
| Approachability | Conversational, second-person ("you") | Stiff, academic, jargon-heavy prose |
| Specificity | Concrete numbers, real examples | Vague claims ("boost engagement", "drive results") |
| Action bias | Lead with what to do, not theory | Passive observations, "it depends" hedging |

### Words to Use

"quiz funnel", "zero-party data", "personalized experience", "interactive content", "completion rate", "product recommendation", "lead qualification", "conversion-optimized", "branded experience", "AI-powered"

### Words to Avoid

"revolutionary", "game-changing", "cutting-edge", "leverage", "synergy", "utilize", "best-in-class", "world-class", "seamless" (without specifics), "robust"

### Example Sentences

> Product recommendation quizzes generate 4-5x higher conversion rates than static collection pages because they match buyers to products before they hit the cart.

> A 7-question lead qualification quiz replaces the 15-field form nobody finishes -- and delivers higher-quality leads to your sales team.

## Content Differentiation

Every Yapform blog post must include at least one of these original value signals:

1. **Real quiz/form performance data** -- Completion rates, conversion lifts, AOV changes from actual Yapform quizzes (anonymized if needed)
2. **AI-specific angle** -- How AI changes quiz creation (auto-generated questions, smart branching, result personalization)
3. **Step-by-step builder walkthrough** -- Screenshots or descriptions of building the quiz/form inside Yapform
4. **Industry-specific examples** -- DTC verticals (skincare, supplements, fashion, home goods) or lead gen verticals (SaaS, financial services, education)

## CTA Strategy

| Funnel Stage | CTA | Placement |
|-------------|-----|-----------|
| Top (awareness) | "Explore quiz templates" or "See interactive content examples" | End of post, after key insight |
| Middle (consideration) | "Try the AI quiz builder free" or "Build your first quiz in 5 minutes" | After product-led section, in-content |
| Bottom (decision) | "Start your free trial" or "Book a demo" | Sticky footer, post conclusion |

**Rule:** One primary CTA per post. Do not stack multiple CTAs. Match CTA to the reader's intent based on the keyword's funnel stage.

## Competitive Positioning

- **Frame as category**: Position Yapform within "AI quiz builders" and "interactive content platforms" -- not generic "form builders"
- **Lead with capability gaps**: Highlight what quizzes do that static forms cannot (personalization, engagement, zero-party data) rather than naming competitors
- **Comparison posts**: When writing "[X] vs [Y]" content, be factual and fair. State objective feature differences. Never disparage competitors
- **Differentiation anchors**: AI-powered generation, DTC-native integrations, conversion-optimized templates, personalization engine
